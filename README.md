
Privacy Policy — AutoDo
Last updated: May 20, 2026

AutoDo is a free Android automation app with optional in-app purchases, built by Beethoven Hrangkhawl.

1. Information We Collect
AutoDo is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

Google account email and user ID — only if you choose to sign in with Google. This is optional.
Automation data — all profiles, triggers, and actions you create are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to any server.

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics of our own.

2. How We Use Information

Your Google Sign-In email and user ID are used solely to identify your account for Pro subscription restoration across devices.
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.
All automation data remains on your device at all times.


3. Permissions We Use
AutoDo requests the following permissions and uses them only for the stated purposes:

Location — to detect when you enter or exit a geographic area for location-based automation triggers. Location data is processed on-device and never sent to our servers.
Bluetooth — to detect Bluetooth device connections and disconnections for automation triggers.
Phone and Call State — to detect incoming calls for call-received automation triggers.
Call Log — to support call-related automation triggers. This data is read on-device only and never transmitted.
SMS — to send text messages as part of an automation action, only when explicitly configured by you.
Contacts — to allow you to select a contact when setting up an SMS action. Contact data is never stored or transmitted.
Notifications — to send you automation notifications as part of an action you configured.
Calendar — to read your calendar events for date and event-based automation triggers.
Usage Stats — to detect when a specific app is launched, for app launch automation triggers.
Accessibility Service — AutoDo uses Android's Accessibility Service to toggle WiFi, mobile data, and Bluetooth on your behalf through the quick settings panel. This service acts only on your explicit instructions and does not read, record, or transmit any screen content, text, or personal information from your device.


4. Third Party Services
AutoDo uses the following third party services, each of which operates under its own privacy policy:

Google AdMob — used to display advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalisation. See Google's Privacy Policy.
Google Play Billing — used to process in-app purchases securely. See Google's Privacy Policy.
Google Sign-In — used for optional account identification. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Pro users and users who have removed ads will see no advertisements.

5. Data Storage and Security
All data created within AutoDo — including your automation profiles, triggers, and actions — is stored exclusively in a local database on your device using Android's Room database and DataStore. This data never leaves your device through our systems.

6. Data Deletion
Since all automation data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time simply by uninstalling the app. To request removal of any Google Sign-In association or account information, contact us at the email address below and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Children
AutoDo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
