AutoDo Privacy Policy (v1.3.0)
Effective Date: June 28, 2026
AutoDo is an Android automation app with optional in-app purchases, built by Bee'sGeniusLab. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect
AutoDo is designed with privacy in mind. We collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

Google Account Information: If you choose to sign in with Google (optional), we collect your email address, user ID, and profile photo URL. This is used solely for account identification and subscription management.

Automation Data:

Free Users: All profiles, triggers, and actions you create are stored locally on your device only.

Pro Users: To support cross-device synchronization and data restoration, your automation profiles are synced to our secure cloud database (Firebase Firestore).

Usage Statistics: We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data (e.g., which features are used most) and crash reports. This helps us identify bugs and improve the app. This data does not include your personal automation configurations or private data.

2. How We Use Information
Account Management: Your Google account details are used to identify your account, restore Pro subscriptions, and facilitate cloud sync for Pro users.

Automation Execution: Your data is used solely to execute the automation rules you have configured.

App Improvement: Anonymous analytics help us monitor app performance and stability.
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.

3. Permissions We Use
AutoDo requests the following sensitive permissions to enable specific automation features:

Location (including Background Location): To detect when you enter or exit a geographic area for location-based triggers. This requires background access to function even when the app is not actively open.

Accessibility Service: Used to perform system actions like toggling Wi-Fi, Mobile Data, Bluetooth, and Locking the Screen on your behalf. This service acts only on your explicit instructions and does not read, record, or transmit any screen content, text, or personal information.

Phone & Call Log: To detect incoming calls for call-received automation triggers.

SMS: To send text messages as part of an automation action you explicitly configure.

Camera: Used solely to toggle the device flashlight as an automation action.

Contacts: To allow you to select a contact when setting up SMS actions or call filters.

Usage Stats & Overlay: To detect when specific apps are launched (for app-launch triggers) and to display notifications or launch apps over other applications.

System Settings: To modify screen brightness, screen timeout, and auto-rotation settings as part of your automations.

Bluetooth & Calendar: To detect Bluetooth device connections and read calendar events for relevant automation 
triggers.

4. Third-Party Services
AutoDo uses the following third-party services, which operate under their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob & Unity Ads: Used to display advertisements to free users. They may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization.

Google Play Billing: Used to process in-app purchases securely.

Google Firebase: Used for secure cloud storage (Firestore), authentication, and anonymous analytics.

Google User Messaging Platform (UMP): Used to manage user consent for data usage and advertisements.

5. Data Storage and Security
Local data is stored securely using Android's Room database and DataStore. Cloud data for Pro users is encrypted and stored on Google Firebase servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

6. Data Deletion
Local Data: You can delete all local data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app cache.

Cloud Data: Pro users can request the deletion of their cloud-synced data and Google account association by contacting us at the email address below. We will process such requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy
AutoDo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
