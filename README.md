# AutoDo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

AutoDo is a free Android automation app with optional in-app purchases built by Beethoven Hrangkhawl.

## Information We Collect
- Google account email (only if you choose to sign in with Google)
- All automation profiles, triggers and actions are stored locally on your device only

## How We Use Information
- Google Sign-In email is used only to sync your Pro subscription across devices
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties
- All automation data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers

## Third Party Services
This app uses the following third party services:
- Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements
- Google Play Billing - for processing in-app purchases
- Google Sign-In - for optional account sync

Each service has its own privacy policy.

## Permissions We Use
- Location - for location-based automation triggers
- Phone/Call state - for call-received triggers
- SMS - for send SMS actions
- Contacts - for selecting contacts in SMS actions
- Notifications - for sending automation notifications
- Calendar - for calendar event triggers
- Usage stats - for app launch detection

## Children
This app is not directed at children under 13.
